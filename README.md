# Costel's Discord Escape 🎮

A browser-based roguelike card game inspired by Slay the Spire. Play as **Costel**, who must survive gaming sessions with his friends without tilting and rage-quitting Discord.

## Play Now

👉 **[Play the game](https://florincojocariuwork-cyber.github.io/Discord-Escape-The-Costel-Run/)**

## How to Play

- **😤 Tilt** is your health bar. Enemies raise it. Hit 100 → Costel disconnects.
- **⚡ Energy** resets to 3 each turn. Spend it to play cards.
- **⚔️ Attack cards** deal Chill damage to enemies (reduces their Determination).
- **🌬️ Skill cards** reduce Tilt, gain Block, or draw cards.
- **💪 Power cards** give permanent combat effects.
- Navigate the map, fight enemies, visit shops, rest, and find events.
- Defeat all 3 act bosses to win!

## Hosting on GitHub Pages

1. Go to your repo **Settings → Pages**
2. Source: **Deploy from branch**
3. Branch: `main`, Folder: `/ (root)`
4. Click Save — site is live in ~2 minutes
